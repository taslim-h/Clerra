Our Purpose
Born to Care
for Fabric &
the Planet.

Clerra emerged from a simple belief — that everyday fabric care can be a force for good. As an ACI Group brand, we blend artisanal Bangladeshi fragrance traditions with SHIELD+ fiber science, creating detergents that protect your clothes while respecting our rivers, our communities, and future generations.
